# Ecosystem Problems & Product Motivation

This document supplements the master plan without replacing it.
It explains why Arcaea-Viewer exists in terms of real player friction and real community gaps around Arcaea.

## 1. Why This Project Exists

Arcaea-Viewer exists to improve the player experience around Arcaea.
The game is strong on atmosphere and mechanics, but the surrounding ecosystem still leaves players with too much scattered knowledge, too little coherent progression support, and too few immersive companion tools.

The project should therefore feel like a long-term companion space for Arcaea players rather than a technically impressive collection of systems.

## 2. Ecosystem Problems

### 2.1 Fragmented Knowledge

Important Arcaea information is scattered across:

- X/Twitter
- Discord
- Reddit
- spreadsheets
- old community knowledge
- unofficial resources

As a result, new and returning players often do not know:

- what to play
- what to buy
- what charts fit their skill level
- how progression actually works
- how lore connects together

Arcaea-Viewer should help consolidate that knowledge into a clearer and more usable companion experience.

### 2.2 Weak Long-Term Retention

Arcaea does not naturally create strong daily retention loops outside the game itself.

Players can love the game and still drift away because there is little ambient presence, little reason to return between sessions, and few companion surfaces that keep the game emotionally present.

Arcaea-Viewer should help create a place that players want to revisit even when they are not actively playing.

### 2.3 Progression Friction

Difficulty labels alone are not enough for meaningful progression planning.

Players often struggle to:

- identify practical next goals
- understand relative chart difficulty
- discover training paths
- find charts matching specific mechanics

The product should reduce this friction with progression tools that are practical, explainable, and grounded in real player use.

### 2.4 Replay and Learning Accessibility

Replay learning and performance understanding are difficult and fragmented.

There is little immersive tooling for:

- understanding misses
- analyzing timing
- comparing runs
- watching patterns clearly

Replay features should therefore focus on learning accessibility, not just raw technical evaluation.

### 2.5 Lore Discoverability

Lore is emotionally important, but many players experience it as fragments rather than as a coherent journey.

Story fragments, packs, characters, and gameplay context are often disconnected.

A stronger lore explorer should help players move through that material in a more coherent, memorable, and emotionally legible way.

### 2.6 Update and Event Discoverability

News, patches, events, and important updates are often easy to miss unless users actively monitor social platforms.

That creates unnecessary friction for players who want to stay informed without living on social media.

Arcaea-Viewer can help present important updates in a calmer, more durable companion format.

## 3. Product Motivation

Arcaea-Viewer is not trying to replace the game, redesign Arcaea itself, become a social media platform, become a research project, or become an enterprise ecosystem.

It should remain:

- grounded
- fanmade
- immersive
- practical
- emotionally aligned with Arcaea players

The project should feel like a long-term companion space that understands how Arcaea players actually think, learn, remember, and return.

## 4. How This Shapes the Product

### 4.1 Progression Systems

Progression systems should directly address progression friction.
They should help players choose goals, compare chart difficulty, and find useful training paths.

### 4.2 Lore Explorer

Lore exploration should directly address fragmented discovery.
It should connect story, packs, characters, and gameplay context in a way that feels coherent rather than scattered.

### 4.3 Replay Viewer

Replay viewing should directly address learning accessibility.
It should make misses, timing, and pattern reading easier to understand in an immersive, legible way.

### 4.4 Community Surfaces

Community-oriented features should emphasize fandom warmth instead of engagement metrics.
They should feel intimate, curated, atmospheric, memory-oriented, and useful.

They should not drift into infinite feeds, algorithmic engagement systems, or generic social platform behavior.

### 4.5 Update Surfaces

Update and event surfaces should make important information easier to notice without turning the product into a noisy notification system.

## 5. Design Consequences

This motivation layer implies a few product rules:

- prioritize clarity over clutter
- prefer curated surfaces over high-volume feeds
- use atmosphere to support emotional attachment
- make progression and learning tools directly useful
- keep the product intimate rather than performative
- treat discovery as a companion function, not a growth hack

## 6. Relationship to the Master Plan

This document is additive.
It explains the real ecosystem problems that justify the product direction, while the master plan remains the main source for structure, scope, and implementation planning.
